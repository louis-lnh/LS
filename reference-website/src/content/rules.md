# SHD Lifesteal Rules

Welcome to SHD Lifesteal.

These rules exist to keep the event competitive, fair, and enjoyable for everyone. Attempting to bypass the spirit of the rules through loopholes, technicalities, or exploit abuse may still result in punishment.

---

# 1. Player Conduct

## Respect Other Players

Treat other players with basic respect.

Competitive trash talk is expected, but the following are not allowed:

* Harassment
* Hate Speech
* Discrimination
* Real-Life Threats
* Doxxing
* Excessive Personal Attacks

Fight in-game, not in real life.

---

## Cheating & Unfair Advantages

Any modification, software, exploit, or method that provides an unfair advantage is prohibited.

Examples include:

* X-Ray
* Kill Aura
* Reach
* Trigger Bot
* Auto Clickers
* Fly Hacks
* ESP
* Freecam
* Minimap mods
* World map mods
* Packet Manipulation
* Duplication Exploits
* Gameplay Automation Macros

If you are unsure whether something is allowed, ask staff before using it.

---

## Exploit Abuse

Abusing bugs, unintended mechanics, loopholes, or technical oversights is prohibited.

Known exploits should be reported.

Staff may remove items, reverse actions, or issue punishments if exploits are abused.

---

## Alternate Accounts

Alternate accounts may not be used for any competitive advantage.

This includes:

* Heart Farming
* Scouting
* Extra Storage
* Ban Evasion
* Bypassing Restrictions

---

# 2. Lifesteal System

## Hearts

* Every player starts with 10 Hearts.
* The maximum is 20 Hearts.
* Players lose 1 Heart when they die.
* Players gain 1 Heart when they kill another player.

---

## Elimination

A player who dies while already on 1 Heart is eliminated.

Eliminated players may not:

* Continue playing
* Spectate the server
* Scout bases
* Share information for active players

A player may only return through the official revival system.

---

## Heart Items

Heart Items may be obtained through:

* Player kills
* Heart withdrawals
* Official recipes

Heart Items can be consumed to gain Hearts up to the maximum limit of 20.

---

## Heart Storage

Heart Items may only be stored:

* In a player's inventory
* Dropped in the world
* Displayed in item frames

Heart Items may not be stored inside containers.

Examples include:

* Chests
* Barrels
* Shulker Boxes
* Ender Chests
* Hoppers
* Bundles
* Any other storage block

---

# 3. Grace Period

## Grace Duration

The server begins with a 60-minute Grace Period.

---

## During Grace Period

The following are disabled:

* PvP
* Combat Tagging
* Lifesteal
* Heart Loss
* Eliminations
* Heart Drops
* Revival Crafting
* Revival Usage

Deaths during Grace Period do not remove Hearts.

---

# 4. Combat Rules

## Combat Tag

Entering PvP places both players in combat for 30 seconds.

Taking or dealing damage refreshes the timer.

---

## Combat Logging

Logging out while combat tagged is prohibited.

Combat loggers are treated as dead.

Heart loss, elimination, inventory loss, and kill credit may still apply.

---

## Pearl Stasis Chambers

Pearl Stasis Chambers are allowed.

However:

* Activating a stasis chamber while combat tagged is prohibited.
* Using another player to pull you out of combat through a stasis chamber is prohibited.

Combat must be escaped through normal gameplay.

---

## Elytra

Elytras receive a combat cooldown after PvP interactions.

Attempting to bypass combat flight restrictions is prohibited.

---

## Riptide Tridents

Riptide Tridents may not be used while combat tagged.

---

## Combat Mobility Bypasses

Ender Pearls, Elytras, and Riptide Tridents may not be used to create combat kills while restricted by the combat system.

Attempting to bypass cooldowns or combat restrictions is prohibited.

Examples include:

* Flying above a player, swapping from Elytra to chestplate, and dropping into a Mace kill.
* Using Riptide, Ender Pearls, Elytra flight, or spear-style airborne attacks to bypass combat restrictions.
* Setting up mobility-assisted kills that avoid the intended combat cooldown.

Staff may treat suspicious mobility-assisted kills as anti-cheat or rules cases even if the mod does not catch every setup automatically.

---

# 5. Revival System

## Revivals

Eliminated players may only return through the official revival system.

Revived players return with 3 Hearts.

---

## Revival Abuse

Attempting to bypass revival requirements, cooldowns, or systems is prohibited.

---

# 6. Raiding & Bases

## Raiding

Raiding is fully allowed.

Players may:

* Steal loot
* Break blocks
* Infiltrate bases
* Eliminate defenders

---

## Base Defense

Players may use:

* Walls
* Hidden Bases
* Secret Entrances
* Traps
* Defensive Redstone

provided they do not violate other rules.

---

## Griefing

Destroying builds solely to ruin them is prohibited.

Raiding should serve a competitive purpose.

---

# 7. Explicitly Allowed Mechanics

The following mechanics are allowed:

* Nether Roof Travel
* AFK Farms
* Automatic Farms
* Villager Trading
* Iron Farms
* Gold Farms
* Mob Farms
* Chunk Loaders
* Pearl Stasis Chambers (outside combat)
* Bed Mining
* Bed Explosions
* Large Alliances
* Betrayal
* Scamming
* TNT Duping for farming purposes

Unless stated otherwise, vanilla mechanics are generally allowed.

---

# 8. Restricted & Disabled Features

The following are disabled or restricted:

## Combat & PvP

* End Crystals
* Respawn Anchors
* TNT Minecarts

---

## Survival

* Totems of Undying
* Tipped Arrows

---

## Equipment

The following Netherite equipment is disabled:

* Netherite Sword
* Netherite Axe
* Netherite Helmet
* Netherite Chestplate
* Netherite Leggings
* Netherite Boots

Netherite tools such as pickaxes, shovels, and hoes remain allowed.

---

## Maces

Only two custom Maces exist for the season.

These Maces are event rewards and cannot be obtained through normal crafting.

Crafted, duplicated, renamed, modified, disenchanted, or otherwise bypassed Maces are not permitted and may be removed by staff or server systems.

---

## Enchantments

Maximum allowed enchantments:

* Protection III
* Sharpness IV

Anything above these limits is not permitted.

---

# 9. Potion Restrictions

The following effects are prohibited:

* Poison
* Slowness
* Mining Fatigue
* Nausea
* Blindness
* Wither
* Levitation
* Instant Damage
* Slow Falling

---

## Strength

* Strength I is allowed.
* Strength II and higher are prohibited.

---

## Weakness

Normal Weakness Potions may be used for curing Zombie Villagers.

Splash and Lingering Weakness Potions are prohibited.

---

## Turtle Master

Turtle Master Potions are allowed.

---

# 10. Machines & Performance

## Lag Machines

Any machine intentionally designed to cause lag, crashes, or instability is prohibited.

Examples include:

* Entity Spam
* Item Spam
* Excessive Redstone Abuse
* Chunk Crash Attempts

---

## TNT Duping

TNT Duping is allowed only for farming and resource gathering.

Examples:

* World Eaters
* Quarry Systems
* Resource Farms

TNT Duping may not be used for:

* PvP Traps
* Base Traps
* Automatic Kill Systems
* Combat Applications

---

# 11. Teams & Alliances

## Team Size

There is no team size limit.

Players may play:

* Solo
* In Small Groups
* In Large Alliances

---

## Betrayal & Scamming

Betrayal, lying, backstabbing, and scamming are allowed.

However, they may not cross into:

* Harassment
* Real-Life Threats
* Exploit Abuse
* Other prohibited behavior

---

# 12. Staff Authority

## Common Sense Rule

Not every situation can be covered by written rules.

Attempting to abuse loopholes or technicalities may still result in punishment.

---

## Staff Decisions

Staff decisions are final.

---

## Balance Changes

The SHD Team may adjust:

* Recipes
* Cooldowns
* Mechanics
* Disabled Features
* Event Systems

when required for balance, fairness, or server stability.

---

# Final Rule

Play to win.

Do not cheat.

Do not exploit.

Do not intentionally lag or crash the server.

If you have to ask yourself whether something is probably against the rules, it probably is.
